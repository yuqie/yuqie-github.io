J-1签证申请

Posted on 2019-07-31 In Text Views:

1. 收到DS-2019

2. 在线填写DS-160（需要一张2英寸*2英寸的6个月以内的照片）

     照片要求：

![](https://travel.state.gov/content/travel/en/us-visas/visa-information-resources/photos/photo-composition-template/_jcr_content/tsg-rwd-content-page-parsysxxx/image_9868421.img.jpg/1512148995443.jpg)

![](https://travel.state.gov/content/travel/en/us-visas/visa-information-resources/photos/photo-composition-template/_jcr_content/tsg-rwd-content-page-parsysxxx/image.img.jpg/1512148997050.jpg)

  • 彩色，6个月以内免冠照  

  • 背景：纯白或者米黄色（plain white or off-white）

  • 衣服：normally wear on a daily basis（不能穿制服）

  • 不能戴眼镜

  • 像素要求：miminum 600*600 pixels，maximum 1200*1200 pixels.

      https://travel.state.gov/content/travel/en/us-visas/visa-information-resources/photos/photo-composition-template.html
